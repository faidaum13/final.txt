<h2>do's</h2>
<strong>drowning:</strong>
<ul>
<li>go get help or go help</li>
<li>move to safer place</li>
<li>check if he/she is conscious</li>
<li>CPR (this CPR shown is for child only not for adult: 5 rescue breaths, 30 compressions, 2 rescue breaths again. [keep going till it went okay] reminding to push hard and fast to revive easily</li>
</ul>
<strong>poisoning:</strong>
<ul>
<li>take away the poison</li>
<li>make them spit if still not digested</li>
<li>call poison help hotline</li>
</ul>
<strong>swallowing of coin:</strong>
<ul>
<li>bend the person over, at the waist to face the ground. strike 5 separate times between the person's shoulder blades with the heel of your head.</li>
<li>give 5 abdominal thrust</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<strong>stair fall</strong>
<li>calm down</li>
<li>check for bleeding(external)</li>
<li>take to hospital for checking of internal bleed</li>
<li>use ice packs</li>
<li>look for other injuries</li>
<li>look for painful or breathing difficulty</li>

<h2>don'ts</h2>
<strong>drowning:</strong>
<ul>
<li>don't wait before rescuing</li>
</ul>
<strong>poisoning:</strong>
<ul>
<li>don't make the child vomit</li>
</ul>
<strong>swallowing of coin:</strong>
<ul>
<li>don't try to get the coin using your finger</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<strong>stair fall</strong>
<li>don't over work</li>
<li>avoid carrying heavy things</li>
