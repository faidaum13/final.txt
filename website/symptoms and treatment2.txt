<p>
<h2>Symptoms</h2>
Measles:
<ul>
<li>fever</li>
<li>cold</li>
<li>watery eyes</li>
</ul>
Malaria:
<ul>
<li>fever</li>
<li>headache</li>
<li>chills</li>
<li>nausea</li>
</ul>
Pneumonia:
<ul>
<li>fever</li>
<li>chills</li>
<li>Nausea</li>
<li>cough and phlegm</li>
<li>Blood in sputum</li>
<li>chest pain</li>
</ul>

<h2>Treatment</h2>
Measles:(note: there is no specific treatment for this kind of disease but these following are possible treatment)
<ul>
<li>rest(avoid busy works)</li>
<li>drinking fluids</li>
<li>OTC medicine for fever</li>
<li>Antibiotics</li>
<li>Vitamin A</li>
</ul>
Malaria:
<ul>
<li>Milk</li>
<li>Tea</li>
<li>Apple cider vinegar</li>
</ul>
Pneumonia:
<ul>
<li>rest</li>
<li>ginger</li>
<li>honey</li>
<li>grapes</li>
<li>drinking of fluids</li>
</ul>
